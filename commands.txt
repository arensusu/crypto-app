funding: get the funding rate history of pair
perp: get the perpetual data of pair
show: show the watchlist of pair
addfunding: add a pair into watchlist of funding rate. Bot will send message when funding rate flips
addprice: add a pair into watchlist of price. Bot will send message when price touches your target price
removefunding: remove a pair from watchlist of funding rate
removeprice: remove a pair from watchlist of price
